Who is Jade?

Jade was born in a small town just outside of Manchester England called Glossop. At the age of six, her family immigrated to the Northern California town of Clayton. On her 18th birthday, Jade earned dual citizenship with the United Kingdom and the United States.

Jade is a Radiology Technologist for the Wahoo Nebraska based hospital Saunders County Medical Center. Jade specializes in Computerized Tomography and Diagnostic Radiography.

During her time at the University of Nebraska at Lincoln, Jade met her husband, Michael Lohr. The couple married in 2018, in a Castle, in Glennwood Iowa.

In November of 2020, Jade and Mike welcomed their son Colin into their lives. Colin is the spitting image of his mom and is her entire world.

Jade earned her Associates degree from Southeast Community College, in 2017, which is located in Lincoln Nebraska.

Upon earning her degree, Jade started working for Bryan Medical Center, also located in Lincoln Nebraska. Jade started with the modality of X-Ray and soon mastered that position. Early 2020, Jade began the journey of learning Computerized Tomorgraphy. Upon successive trainings, Jade now finds herself wanting more and will soon be learning the modality of MRI.

Jade has many hobbies, some that include reading, gazing up in the night sky, watching her favorite team Manchester City's Football Club, hanging out with friends and family, or just relaxing.

Nowadays you can find Jade on her small farm in Wahoo, Nebraska raising dogs, chickens and worms.
